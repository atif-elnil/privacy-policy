# Privacy Policy — Antihypertensives
**Last updated: April 2026 · Version 1.0**

## Summary
Antihypertensives does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The app functions entirely offline. There are no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and no network requests of any kind.

## At a glance
- No data collected from you
- No internet connection required or used
- No account or login
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No third-party data sharing

## Data stored on your device
Antihypertensives stores the following data locally on your device only using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device:
- Display preferences — font size setting
- Disclaimer acknowledgement — whether you have accepted the medical disclaimer

## Network activity
Antihypertensives makes zero network requests. All drug data and clinical content is bundled within the app. The app works fully in Airplane Mode.

## Target audience
This application is intended for qualified healthcare professionals aged 18 and over. It is not directed at children.

## Third-party services
Antihypertensives does not integrate any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or crash-reporting tools.

## Medical disclaimer
Antihypertensives is a clinical reference tool for qualified healthcare professionals. It is not a diagnostic tool and does not constitute medical advice. Always verify doses against your local formulary.
